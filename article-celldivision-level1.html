Intro to Cell Division

Shared Topic • Level 1 (Class 9)

Cells don't live forever. They need to divide to make new cells.

Why Divide?

  • Growth: To make the organism bigger.
  • Repair: To replace old or damaged cells.
  • Reproduction: To create offspring.

There are two main types: Mitosis (for growth) and Meiosis (for reproduction).
