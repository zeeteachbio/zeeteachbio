C3 and C4 Pathways

Shared Topic • Level 3 (Class 11)

The dark reaction (Biosynthetic phase) of photosynthesis occurs in the stroma.

C3 Pathway (Calvin Cycle)

The first stable product is a 3-carbon compound (PGA). Occurs in most plants.

C4 Pathway

The first stable product is a 4-carbon compound (OAA). Occurs in plants adapted to dry tropical regions (e.g., Maize, Sorghum).
