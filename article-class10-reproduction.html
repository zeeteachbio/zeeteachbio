How do Organisms Reproduce?

Class 10 • Chapter 8

Reproduction is the process by which living organisms produce new individuals of the same species.

Asexual Reproduction

Single parent involved. Offspring are clones. (e.g., Fission in Amoeba, Budding in Hydra).

Sexual Reproduction

Two parents involved. Genetic variation occurs. (e.g., Humans, Flowering Plants).
