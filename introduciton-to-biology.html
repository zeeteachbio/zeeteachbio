### **Introduction**

Biology is a branch of natural sciences that deals with the study of living beings. It provides knowledge about living organisms, which differ from each other in shape, size, and composition. The word *Biology* comes from the Greek words **“Bios”** meaning **life** and **“Logos”** meaning **study or reasoning**. Thus, biology means **the study of life**.


---


### **What is Life?**

Life cannot be defined properly, but it can be identified through various life processes or properties of living organisms.


---


### **Life Properties (with brief definitions)**


**Digestion:**  

The process of breaking down complex food substances into simpler, absorbable forms.


**Respiration:**  

The process of releasing energy from food, usually in the presence of oxygen.


**Metabolism:**  

The sum of all chemical reactions occurring within a living organism.


**Movement:**  

The ability of an organism to change its position or the movement of body parts.


**Growth:**  

A permanent increase in size due to cell division and cell enlargement.


**Development:**  

Changes that occur during the life cycle of an organism, leading to maturity.


**Excretion:**  

The removal of metabolic waste products from the body.


**Irritability (Response to Stimuli):**  

The ability of an organism to detect and respond to changes in the environment.


**Reproduction:**  

The process through which living organisms produce new individuals of their own kind.
