Biodiversity and Conservation

Class 12 • Chapter 15

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life forms on earth.

Levels

Genetic, Species, and Ecological diversity.

Conservation

  • In-situ: Conservation in natural habitat (National Parks).
  • Ex-situ: Conservation outside natural habitat (Zoos, Seed Banks).
