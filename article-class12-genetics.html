Class 12 • Chapter 6

DNA is the genetic material in most organisms.

Central Dogma

DNA -> RNA -> Protein.

Processes

  • Replication: Copying DNA.
  • Transcription: DNA to mRNA.
  • Translation: mRNA to Protein.
