Cell Division: Mitosis

Shared Topic • Level 2 (Class 10)

Mitosis is equational division where the chromosome number remains the same in daughter cells.

Significance

  • Responsible for growth of multicellular organisms.
  • Helps in cell repair and replacement.
  • Maintains genetic stability.

It consists of Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, and Telophase.
