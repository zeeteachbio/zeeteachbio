1.4.1 describe the roles and responsibilities of professionals

1.4 Careers in Biology

1.4.1 describe the roles and responsibilities of professionals working in medicine and surgery, fisheries, agriculture, animal husbandry, horticulture, farming and forestry (FA)

Medicine and Surgery

Role: Doctors diagnose, treat and prevent diseases, while surgeons perform operations to repair or remove damaged tissues.
Responsibilities: Caring for patients, prescribing medicines, performing surgeries safely, maintaining hygiene, and saving lives during emergencies.

Fisheries

Role: Fisheries professionals manage fish breeding, conservation, and harvesting.
Responsibilities: Maintaining fish farms, monitoring water quality, improving fish production, protecting aquatic life, and ensuring sustainable fishing practices.

Agriculture

Role: Agricultural scientists and workers grow crops and improve food production.
Responsibilities: Selecting good seeds, managing soil and fertilizers, controlling pests, using modern techniques, and ensuring a healthy crop yield.

Animal Husbandry

Role: Professionals take care of domestic animals for milk, meat, wool, and other products.
Responsibilities: Feeding animals properly, providing shelter, vaccination, disease control, breeding healthy livestock, and improving animal productivity.

Horticulture

Role: Horticulturists grow fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants.
Responsibilities: Maintaining gardens, nurseries, and orchards, improving plant varieties, controlling pests, and producing healthy fruits and flowers.

Farming

Role: Farmers cultivate land and raise animals for food production.
Responsibilities: Ploughing, sowing, irrigating, harvesting crops, maintaining fields, managing farm animals, and using improved agricultural methods.

Forestry

Role: Foresters manage forests and protect wildlife habitats.
Responsibilities: Planting and conserving trees, preventing deforestation, controlling forest fires, protecting biodiversity, and ensuring sustainable use of forest resources.
