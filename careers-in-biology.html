1.1.2 Careers in Biology

Medicine and Surgery:

Medicine involves the diagnosis and treatment of diseases, while surgery is concerned with the repair, replacement, or removal of affected organs.

Agriculture:

This field deals with the production of different varieties of crops, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products. As Pakistan is an agricultural country, this field plays an important role.

Horticulture:

A branch of agriculture that focuses on developing new varieties of plants and improving their products.

Forestry:

Forests support diverse species of plants and animals. This field involves the development of new forests and the preservation of existing ones.

Farming:

This profession includes the development of different kinds of farms such as fish farms, cattle farms, and poultry farms. Modern technologies are used to improve the production of meat, milk, leather, wool, etc.

Animal Husbandry:

A part of agricultural science that deals with the care and breeding of animals that are useful to humans.

Fisheries

This profession focuses on increasing the quantity and quality of fish production. It plays an important role in providing high-protein food, as fish is one of the best sources of protein. Fisheries experts work on fish breeding, fish farming, and improving methods of catching and preserving fish.

Biotechnology

Biotechnology is a very important and sensitive field. It involves the manipulation of genes to produce valuable chemical substances.

Through biotechnology, useful products like insulin, growth hormones, and interferon are produced from bacteria and other organisms.

This field has applications in medicine, agriculture, and industry.
