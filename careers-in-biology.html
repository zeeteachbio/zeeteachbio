1.1.2 Careers in Biology

Biology is a subject that has a wide scope of careers for students. Some of the careers are:

Medicine and Surgery

Medicine deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases. Surgery deals with the repair, replacement, or removal of body parts. These professions are adopted after MBBS.

Fisheries

Deals with the production of fish. Professionals work in the Fisheries Department of Pakistan.

Agriculture

Deals with crops and livestock. Agriculturists work for the betterment of crops like wheat, rice, corn, etc., and livestock like buffalo, cow, etc.

Animal Husbandry

Deals with the care and breeding of domestic animals (livestock) like cattle, sheep, etc.

Biotechnology

Deals with the use of living organisms to make useful products for the welfare of mankind.

Horticulture

Deals with the art of gardening. Horticulturists work for the betterment of existing varieties and the production of new varieties of ornamental plants and fruit plants.

Farming

Deals with the development and maintenance of different types of farms. For example, in some farms, animal breeding technologies are used for the production of animals that are better sources of protein and milk. In poultry farms, chicken and eggs are produced. In fruit farms, different fruit plants are grown.

Forestry

Professionals look after natural forests and advise the government on planting and growing artificial forests.
