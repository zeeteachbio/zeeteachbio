Control and Coordination

Class 10 • Chapter 7

Living organisms respond to stimuli in their environment. This is controlled by the nervous system and hormones.

Nervous System

Consists of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. The neuron is the structural unit.

Reflex Arc

A rapid, automatic response to a stimulus (e.g., pulling hand away from heat).
