Cell Cycle and Division

Shared Topic • Level 3 (Class 11)

The sequence of events by which a cell duplicates its genome and divides.

Phases

  • Interphase: G1, S (Synthesis), and G2 phases.
  • M Phase: Mitosis or Meiosis.

Checkpoints

Control mechanisms that ensure proper division (G1/S, G2/M, and Spindle checkpoints).
