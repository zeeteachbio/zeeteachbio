The Fundamental Unit of Life

Class 9 • Chapter 5

Cells are the basic structural and functional units of life. All living organisms are made up of cells.

Discovery of the Cell

Robert Hooke discovered cells in 1665 while observing a cork slice under a primitive microscope.

Types of Organisms

  • Unicellular: Single-celled (e.g., Amoeba).
  • Multicellular: Many cells (e.g., Humans, Plants).
