Breathing and Exchange of Gases

Class 11 • Chapter 17

Breathing is the exchange of oxygen from the atmosphere with carbon dioxide produced by the cells.

Respiratory Organs

Gills (Fish), Tracheal tubes (Insects), Lungs (Humans).

Mechanism

Inspiration (Inhalation) and Expiration (Exhalation).
