1. Introduction to Biology
1.1.1 Define Biology

Biology is the branch of science that studies living organisms and their interactions with the environment. It explains how living things grow, reproduce, adapt, and perform life processes.

1.1.2 Differentiate Among Botany, Zoology and Microbiology

Botany

Botany is the division of biology that deals with the study of plants, including their structure, functions, growth, and classification.

Zoology:

Zoology is the study of animals, their structure, functions, habitats, and behavior.

Microbiology:

Microbiology is the study of microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa, which are too small to be seen without a microscope.
